## 1. Commissions and payouts

- **Percentage.** You'll earn a recurring **30% commission** on the net value of every TerminalSync Pro subscription referred through your unique link.
- **Recurrence.** Commissions are paid monthly for as long as the referred user keeps their subscription active and in good standing.
- **Payout threshold.** Payouts are processed once your accumulated balance reaches **$50 USD** or more.
- **Payout cycle.** Payouts are issued within the **first 10 days** of each calendar month.

## 2. Attribution system (cookies)

- **Duration.** We use tracking cookies with a **30-day** lifetime.

- **Transparency.** You must honestly disclose that you are an affiliate and that you receive compensation for recommended sales.
- **Self-referrals.** Using your own affiliate link to obtain discounts on personal subscriptions is not allowed. Such commissions will be automatically voided.
