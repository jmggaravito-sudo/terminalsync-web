# Connector logo assets

Last audited: 2026-07-13.

## License and trademark posture

Most vendor logo SVGs in `public/connectors/` are downloaded from Simple Icons and vendored locally so the catalog does not depend on external CDNs at runtime.

Simple Icons publishes the project under CC0 1.0, but its disclaimer notes that individual icon license/guideline metadata can vary and that trademarks remain the property of their owners. TerminalSync uses these logos nominatively: to identify the corresponding integration/connector, not to imply partnership, certification, sponsorship, or endorsement.

Operational rules:

- Store approved logos locally under `public/connectors/`; do not reference `cdn.simpleicons.org` or other external logo CDNs from frontmatter.
- Do not deform, crop, or use vendor marks as TerminalSync branding.
- If a vendor has brand guidelines, follow them before updating the asset.
- If there is no clear vendor logo, use the TerminalSync curated mark instead of inventing an initials tile.
