## When to use

- You have to write an announcement for the company (change, restructuring, layoffs, big release).
- You need a post-mortem of an incident that doesn't read defensive.
- You write weekly status updates and they don't get read.

## What it does

- Asks the situation, audience (team, leadership, whole company), goal.
- Suggests tone: direct, empathetic, formal, transparent.
- Drafts the message, validates that it doesn't leave critical questions unanswered.
- Reviews "noise": jargon, vagueness, false comfort.

## How to use

1. Tell your AI: *"I have to communicate X to the team using `internal-comms`"*.
2. Answer the questions about audience and goal.
3. Review the draft; adjust tone if needed.
4. The skill warns when the message contains unmet expectations.

## Best for

Founders communicating to their team. Engineering managers writing post-mortems. Anyone whose communication doesn't reach the way it should.
