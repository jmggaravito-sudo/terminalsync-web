## When to use

- Your current landing isn't converting and you don't know where to start.
- You're about to launch a new one and want a sanity check before paying for traffic.
- You're A/B testing 2 versions and need an outside opinion before deciding.

## What it does

Looks at your URL (or HTML you paste) and returns a prioritized report:

- **🔴 Conversion killers** (above-the-fold doesn't communicate what you do in 5 sec, CTA ambiguous, hero confuses)
- **🟡 Notable friction** (8-field form when 2 would do, weak social proof, bad contrast)
- **🟢 Good decisions to keep** (what NOT to touch — because you'll iterate and should know what works)

Each item includes: the problem, the exact fix, and an impact estimate (high / medium / low).

Blocks: doesn't recommend "add testimonials" without reason, doesn't say "improve the copy" without a concrete proposal, doesn't flag "responsive design" as an issue.

## How to use

1. Paste the URL or HTML.
2. Tell it the ICP: *"Early-stage founders, $19/mo ticket, technical audience"*.
3. Returns the prioritized list. Work top-down.

## Best for

Founders writing their own landing, growth teams iterating on PLG, agencies delivering audits to clients.
