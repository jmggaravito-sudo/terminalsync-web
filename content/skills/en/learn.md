## When to use

- You want to learn something new (a language, a framework, a domain) without taking a formal course.
- You're stuck self-learning because you don't know what to do next.
- You want exercises with real corrections, not just "study this for an hour".

## What it does

- Initial diagnostic: gauges your level on what you want to learn.
- Builds a sized study plan based on the time you have.
- Generates exercises adapted to your level; corrects them with structured feedback.
- Adjusts the plan when it sees you're stuck or moving fast.

## How to use

1. Tell your AI: *"I want to learn X using `learn`"*.
2. Answer the diagnostic questions.
3. The skill proposes a plan; you accept or adjust the pace.
4. Each session: theory + exercises + structured feedback.

## Best for

Solo learners with discipline who lack a tutor. Self-taught people stuck because they don't know where they're failing. Anyone preferring 1:1 conversation over generic courses.
