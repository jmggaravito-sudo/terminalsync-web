## When to use

- You're doing outreach to 30 scraped contacts and don't want to send the same template to all.
- Your current DM is 200 words and no one replies.
- You want to test 3 distinct angles before picking the final one.

## What it does

From:
- The contact's profile (bio, recent posts, latest video — whatever your scraper detected)
- Your pitch (what you offer, in 1-2 sentences)
- The call-to-action (what you want them to do after reading)

It returns **3 versions** of the DM:

- **Direct**: 60-80 words, hook + pitch + CTA in that order, no warm-up
- **Curious**: opens with a question about something specific in their work
- **Useful**: offers something free first (insight, resource) with the CTA at the end

Each DM includes **at least 1 specific detail** from the profile that proves it's not spam.

Blocks: never uses "I hope this finds you well", "love your work", "quick question" as subject. No decorative emojis. No vague promises.

## How to use

1. Paste the contact's profile (X URL, YouTube channel, LinkedIn — whatever you have).
2. Paste your pitch in 2 sentences.
3. Tell it the exact CTA: *"I want them to reply 'yes, send info'"* or *"I want them to click the link"*.
4. Returns the 3 versions. Pick which sounds most like you and send.

## Best for

Founders doing partner outreach, growth teams scaling cold email, creators reaching out to other creators for collabs.
