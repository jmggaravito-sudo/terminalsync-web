## When to use

- You asked "help me write the hero", "I need landing copy", "draft a welcome email for me".
- You have to write something that sells and don't want it to sound like generic ChatGPT.

## What it does

Before writing, asks 3 things most prompts skip:

- **Who it's for**: a specific persona, not "early adopters".
- **What pain it removes**: in their words, not buzzwords.
- **What evidence you have**: one quote from a real customer.

Then returns copy in three registers: direct, persuasive, irreverent. You pick which matches your brand and it refines.

Output blocks: never uses "elevate", "unlock", "seamless", "in today's fast-paced world", "imagine if", or decorative emojis.

## How to use

1. Tell it what kind of copy you want: hero, email, ad, landing, product description.
2. Answer the 3 questions (1 minute).
3. It returns 3 different registers. Ask for A/B variants of the one you like.

## Best for

Founders writing their own landing, agencies who want a baseline before client review, devs who hate writing copy.
