## When to use

- You asked for "ads for my spa", "creatives for Meta", "Facebook/Instagram ad ideas".
- You have a product or service and want to test angles without hiring a copywriter.

## What it does

Returns 5 complete ad concepts, each with:

- **Hook** (first 3 words that stop the scroll)
- **Body** (~50-word ad copy with CTA)
- **Visual brief** (what image/video to pair with each concept)
- **Psychological angle** (why that message converts for that audience)

## How to use

1. Describe your business in 1–2 lines: *"Wellness spa for women 35–55 in Buenos Aires, self-care messaging."*
2. The skill returns the 5 numbered concepts.
3. Tell it which one you like and it expands with variants (3 versions per concept for A/B testing).

## Best for

Solo founders running growth themselves, agencies needing a fast baseline to present to clients, e-commerce testing angles before investing in production.
