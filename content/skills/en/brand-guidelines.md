## When to use

- You're writing copy, designing a doc, or building a landing and want the brand applied without thinking about it.
- You have a brand guide PDF and want it to "live" inside the AI.
- You collaborate with people generating content who don't know the guide by heart.

## What it does

- Loads colors, fonts, voice, illustrations from your guide.
- Applies them to outputs: corporate doc, landing copy, social post, slides.
- Validates compliance: warns when an output drifts from the guide ("non-brand color", "outside voice").

## How to use

1. Upload (or describe) your brand guide.
2. The skill stores the conventions.
3. From any task, mention *"using `brand-guidelines`"* and the output comes adapted.

## Best for

Solo founders without a design team who want a consistent visual identity. Marketing leads delegating content generation. Anyone with a brand guide that nobody reads.
