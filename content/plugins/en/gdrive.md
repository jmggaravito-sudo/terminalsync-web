## When to use

- You want your AI to write a document (proposal, report, summary) and **save it to your Google Drive**, without copy-pasting between tools.
- You work your client documents in Drive and want the initial drafting off your plate.
- You want to use your existing Drive files as context for the new document.

## What it does

Bundles two pieces that reinforce each other, in one install:

- **Google Drive (the connector)** creates, reads, and organizes files in your Drive.
- **Doc Co-Authoring (the skill)** writes proposals, reports, and send-ready documents — with coherence, evidence, and tone passes, and its limits (it doesn't replace a lawyer on legally binding contracts).

Installed separately, you write in one place and file in another. Together: *"draft a proposal for Acme from these notes and save it to the Clients folder"* → written and saved.

## How to use

1. Install the Plugin and connect your Google Drive account.
2. Ask: *"write a project progress report from these notes and save it to my Drive"*.
3. Review the document and it lands in the folder you point it to.

## Best for

Business owners, consultants, and small teams who build proposals, reports, and client documents and keep them in Drive. Requires connecting your Google Drive account.
