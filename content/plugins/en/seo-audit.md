## When to use

- You want an SEO audit of your site **without** copy-pasting each page's HTML by hand.
- You have a public URL and want issues ordered by impact, with evidence and a clear verdict.
- You're a business owner or marketer and want an honest first pass, not a generic checklist.

## What it does

Bundles two pieces that reinforce each other, in one install:

- **Firecrawl (the connector)** fetches your pages' clean content so the analysis works on what your site actually shows.
- **SEO Auditor (the skill)** analyzes it and returns blockers first, high-impact fixes, evidence per finding, and a **scored verdict** (🟢/🟡/🔴) — **no ranking guarantees**.

Installed separately, you'd have to know you need both and wire them yourself. Together, it's *"audit my site"* and done.

## How to use

1. Install the Plugin and connect **Firecrawl** with its API key.
2. Ask: *"Audit the SEO of my site https://mysite.com"*.
3. Firecrawl fetches the pages; the SEO Auditor reviews them.
4. You get prioritized fixes, the evidence, and the verdict with the single highest-impact fix to start with.

## Best for

Business owners, marketers, and content teams who want a **real** first SEO audit of their site with no manual technical steps. Requires a Firecrawl API key and works best on public pages.
